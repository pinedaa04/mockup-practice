Prices

Here at Kittens To Go, you can rent a cat for an hour or day. If you had a wonderful time with a cat you can even adopt it! Our cats are filled with love and want to make new friends.

Hourly Rate: $3.5 per cat
Daily Rate: $35 per cat

Adoption

Once you you have finished with your cat playdate, and you wanted to keep that precious cat foreve, when you return that cat let one of our employees know that you would like to fill out some adoption papers. Once you adopted your cat and you were not able to take care of it we would gladly take the cat back.

In order to use our services you must fill out one of our liability forms and prove that you are 21 years or older. You will have to renew the form every six months. You must also have your own liter box.
